🍯 The Meadery — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Crafted by Bruce Wolfe

The short version: The Meadery doesn't collect your data. Everything you enter stays on your device. There's no account, no tracking, and no ads.

What the App Stores

Everything you enter — recipes, batches, gravity readings, journal entries, tasting notes, photos, cost calculations, and nutrient schedules — is saved locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it yourself.

The Backup and Export Feature

When you use the Backup or Share Recipe features, the app creates a file on your device. You decide what to do with that file. The app plays no role in where that file goes after export, and we receive no copy of it.

Camera Access

If you add a photo to a brew journal entry, the app accesses your camera or photo library at that moment. Photos are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded anywhere.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't collect your name, email address, or any personal information
  • We don't track how you use the app
  • We don't show ads or work with advertising networks
  • We don't sell data — there is no data to sell
  • We don't require an account of any kind
  • We don't transmit anything to any server we operate

Third-Party Services

The app loads typeface resources from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) when an internet connection is available. No other third-party services, analytics platforms, or tracking tools are used.

Future Features

The Meadery may in future introduce optional analytics or cloud sync features. Any such features will be strictly opt-in and this policy will be updated before they launch.

Children's Privacy

The Meadery is intended for adults. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13.
